Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

This policy explains what information CV Autopilot ("we", "us") collects and how we use it.

Information you provide

To generate your CV, you paste career details (such as work history, skills, and education) into the tool. This text is sent to our AI provider solely to produce your CV and is returned to you. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not ask for your name, address, or government IDs to use the tool.

Payment information

Payments are processed by Razorpay. When you pay, your UPI, card, or bank details are entered on Razorpay's secure checkout — we never see or store your full payment details. We only receive a payment confirmation (such as a payment ID and order ID) to verify the transaction and unlock your download. Razorpay's handling of your data is governed by their own privacy policy.

How we use your information

  • To generate the CV you requested;
  • To verify your payment and unlock your download;
  • To respond to support or refund requests you send us.

Data retention

The career text you paste is processed to create your CV and is not used to build a profile of you or sold to anyone. We keep basic transaction records (payment ID, amount, date) as needed for accounting and to handle refund or dispute requests.

Third parties

We share data only with the service providers needed to run the product: our AI provider (to generate the CV), Razorpay (to take payment), and our hosting provider. We do not sell your personal data.

Cookies

The site uses only the minimal cookies/scripts required for the payment checkout to function. We do not run advertising trackers.

Your choices

To request deletion of any transaction record we hold about you, or to ask a privacy question, email support@autopilotdollar.com.
