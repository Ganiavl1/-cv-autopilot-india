Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026

CV Autopilot ("we", "us") sells a digital product: an AI-generated, ATS-optimised CV delivered instantly as a downloadable file. This policy explains when refunds apply.

Nature of the product

Each purchase of ₹99 unlocks and lets you download one CV that has already been generated and shown to you as a free, watermarked preview. Because the product is delivered digitally and immediately on payment, it is consumed at the moment of download.

No refunds after download

As you can review the full CV as a free preview before paying, and the unlocked file is delivered instantly, payments are non-refundable once the CV has been unlocked and downloaded. By paying, you acknowledge that you have previewed the output and agree to this.

When we do issue refunds

We will refund your ₹99 in full if:

  • You were charged but the CV failed to unlock or download due to a technical fault on our side, and we are unable to fix it for you; or
  • You were charged more than once for the same single download (duplicate charge); or
  • A payment was deducted but no order was completed (failed/abandoned transaction). Such amounts are typically auto-reversed by your bank or UPI app within 5–7 working days; if not, contact us.

How to request a refund

Email support@autopilotdollar.com within 7 days of the transaction with your payment ID (and order ID if available). Eligible refunds are processed back to your original payment method within 5–7 working days.

Cancellations

This is a one-time, pay-per-download product. There are no subscriptions or recurring charges, so there is nothing to cancel.
